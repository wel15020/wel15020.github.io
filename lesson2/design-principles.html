Design Principles

Marcus Welker

Repetition

Citrix Systems

The use of white text and logos repeats throughout the page making everything look like its all apart of one thing. Even if they are different icons and ideas, the repetition of white links them together.

Alignment

ifixit

The text, boxes and hamburger menu are all aligned to the right. This page looks well designed and its easy to find the search box by starting at the top and working straight down.

Clean

Triumph United

The overuse of white in the background gives the appearance of being clean and sterile. The items being sold stand out from the white becuase the background is not distracting.

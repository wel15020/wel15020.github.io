Tours

There are many things to see and do in Cozumel. Below are some of our favorite tours that we know you will become your favorite as well.

Scooter Ride and Snorkeling

Adults & Kids: $49.00 $44.00 | You Save 10%

Are you ready for an adrenaline filled adventure in Cozumel? Then, you’ve come to the right place! Ride our state-of-the-art electric bikes in a thrilling experience with fantastic sights! The light battery of our bikes will have you gliding effortlessly through pave roads, as your guide shows you the many amazing views along the West shoreline of the island.

After delighting at the beautiful scenery, you’ll reach Sky Reef, where your snorkeling adventure begins! Here you’ll be snorkeling the second largest barrier reef in the world! Our 45-Minutes guided snorkeling tour will show you the colorful spectacle of flora & fauna within the clear turquoise waters of the Caribbean.

Afterwards, you can either return with the group, or stay for Happy Hour! If you choose the latter, you’ll be a short cab ride away from the starting point.

This exciting tour is currently at a discounted price! Book today and get ready for an electrifying adventure on the West Coast of Cozumel!

ATV Adventure

Adults: $109.00 $76.50 Kids: $109.00 $58.50 | You Save 40%

What better way to tour a tropical island like Cozumel than in your own custom 4 passenger Dune Buggy? These little buggers are last generation and they are blast just to drive, as the warm Caribbean sun shines down on you. This an all-inclusive tour takes you to the wild, untamed Eastern shore of Cozumel, where there is virtually no development, only beautiful strands of virgin beach, and nature.

Your friendly tour guide will take you through scenic roads and a drive along Cozumel's eastern coastline where you'll enjoy breath taking views and plenty photo opportunities, including a stop in a beautiful secluded white sand beach. There you can lay under the sun, take a walk along the beach, play some beach volleyball or swim at the beautiful protected cove and more.

Upon return you'll visit the town of El Cedral and its small Mayan ruins called The Church, spend some time with people from the local community and enjoy a delicious snack and learn about their traditions and the main festivity of La Santa Cruz. Once you are back to our meeting point you can participate on a beautiful snorkeling from the shore, along the coastline, guided by a professional Diver Master. Afterwards you'll enjoy delicious Mexican lunch, then relax and spend the rest of your stay enjoying of all the facilities at the Resort such as, beach lounge chairs, showers and changing rooms. Additional services as lockers, towels and more!

Ultimate Jeep Sightseeing

Adults: $90.00 $79.00 Kids: $90.00 $50.00 | You Save 55%

The Ultimate Cozumel Island Expedition! This is the mother of all jeep tours on Cozumel, visiting the most attractions, and offering the most photo opportunities of any island tour in Cozumel. Snorkeling on coral reefs, Mayan ruins, green jungles, a Tequila Factory, virgin white sand beaches, and a Mayan Village, all in one awesome tour!

Enjoy amazing snorkeling at Villa Blanca, one of Cozumel's most beautiful off-shore reefs! Of course, if you don't want to snorkel, you don't have to - you can relax in lounge chairs on the beach and work on your tan!

Take a journey back in time as you delve into the lost civilization of the Mayas at San Gervasio, the largest and most significant Mayan Ruins site in Cozumel!

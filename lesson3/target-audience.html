Target Audience

The target audience for the website is anyone wanting to know what the weather will be like in areas that they currently reside or maybe going to in the near future. The visitors are going to be coming to the website to learn more about the weather and how it will impact their life or experience for that day. Visitors will access the site from computers, tablets and mobile devices.

Personas

  • Commuter - Steve (35), currently commutes 50 miles a day to his manufacturing job. The weather can affect what he wears for the day and how long his commute may take. He is considering a job relocation but wants to know the weather differences in the new location.

  • Vacationer - Karen (41), planning a vacation for her family and would like to go somewhere with nice weather. She wants to go when theres the least chance of bad weather so she doesn't have to speak with the manager.

Scenarios

  • Before leaving the house to go to work, a visitor will check the myWeather.life site to see what the weather will be like that day in order to wear appropriate clothing.
  • A commuter can check the site to see how the weather may affect their driving conditions.
  • While planning a vacation, a visitor can view the weather at their destination in order to plan activities and what to bring.
  • While investigating places to live, a visitor may use the information on the site to determine if the local weather is something they could live with.
  • A student or researcher can visit the site to look up historical weather data to build a predictive trend for a school or research project.

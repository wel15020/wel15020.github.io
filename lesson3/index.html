Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to attract and inform visitors of the weather in their life. Upon visiting the site, they can quickly see how the weather near them is going to affect their life for that day. It will also provide a weekly forecast so that visitors can plan their lives around the weather in the near future. If a visitor is planning a trip or a vacation, they can visit the site to see how the weather at their destination can enhance their experience.
